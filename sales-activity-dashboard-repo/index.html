Data pulled live from HubSpot each time this page opens or a range/rep filter changes. Meetings are credited to whoever booked them (not whoever they're assigned to/hosting), and "Meetings Booked" counts by the date the meeting was created/booked (hs_createdate), not the date it's scheduled for — the two are often days or weeks apart. Single-day ranges show hourly buckets; multi-day ranges show one bucket per calendar day, weekends included. "Enquiries" counts contacts newly assigned (or reassigned/reconverted) to a rep during the period — HubSpot's dedicated Enquiries object exists in this account but isn't reachable from this integration, so contact owner-assignment date is used as the closest available proxy. "Speed to First Contact" and "Enquiries Contacted/Untouched" are name-matched between new leads and this period's call log (no clean lead↔call ID join is exposed by this integration) — treat as a close estimate, not an exact figure. Switch "View" to Sales Agent to drill into one rep's KPIs and per-metric trends.
